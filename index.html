Features

IOU-Money has a user friendly interface which runs smoothly in cross-platforms. Enjoy all features in both ANdoir and iOS with ease. Explore some of the snapshots of our application and have a visual experience of the application whih you will soon be downloading.

View your transaction history as a trailing timeline

We have a feature of adding your friends in your profile. Foe each friends you can see your transaction as a tree like timeline

The tree like transaction timeline goes indefinitely to your first transaction. With this you can observe how much and how frequently you are giving and taking money with your friend.

Visualize the flow of your money with Graphs and Charts.

  • View your monthly, weekly transaction
  • Color separated debit and credit amount with the percentage of amount
  • View the graph and analyze your expenditure you did in a timeframe

List your credits and filter them as Verified and Unverified

IOU-Money provides this feature of listing your debit and credits. Not only listing the transactions but we can also filter our debit and credit as verified and unverified transactions.

Easy Currency Conversion

Choose any currency as your default and you can change anytime you like. If your friend pays you in differeny currency the amount gets converted by the application. You will then see the corresponding amount of your currency.

You can also change your default currency time and again. No hurdles created. The transaction graph, debit and credit history, your transaction tree everywhere those changes will be reflected in a blink of an eye.

User Profile & Friends

IOU-Money displays your debit and credit amount in your user profile. This User profile includes friends in your network.
